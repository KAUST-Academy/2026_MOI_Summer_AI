Summer AI Training

Welcome to the MEng-AI Cohort 3 Summer Training — a three-week journey through Deep Learning, Computer Vision, Sequence Models, and Reinforcement Learning.
Here you can find all of the materials, lectures, and labs.

WEEK 1

Foundations & Computer Vision

Neural network fundamentals, CNNs, and the core computer vision tasks of detection and segmentation.

Day 1 · 28 June

Introduction to Neural Networks

Fundamentals of deep learning, the perceptron, and building neural networks from the ground up.

Day 2 · 29 June

Deep Learning

Practical deep learning: data augmentation, transfer learning, and fine-tuning pretrained models.

Day 3 · 30 June

Convolutional Neural Networks

CNN architectures and image classification, built from scratch and on benchmark datasets.

Day 4 · 1 July

Object Detection

Detecting and localizing objects with the R-CNN and YOLO families of models.

Day 5 · 2 July

Image Segmentation

Semantic and instance segmentation with U-Net, SMP, and YOLO.

WEEK 2

Sequence Models & Modern Architectures

Recurrent networks, Transformers, Vision Transformers, and self-supervised representation learning.

Day 1 · 5 July

Recurrent Neural Networks

Sequence modeling with RNNs, LSTMs, and GRUs.

Day 2 · 6 July

Transformers

Attention mechanisms and the Transformer architecture.

Day 3 · 7 July

Vision Transformers

Applying Transformers to images with the ViT architecture.

Day 4 · 8 July

Self-Supervised Learning

Learning useful representations without labels.

Day 5 · 9 July

Contrastive Learning

Contrastive objectives for representation learning.

WEEK 3

Reinforcement Learning

From RL foundations and value-based methods to policy gradients, continuous control, and advanced topics.

Day 1 · 12 July

RL Foundations & Value-Based Methods

Reinforcement learning fundamentals with Q-learning, SARSA, and DQN.

Day 2 · 13 July

Policy Gradient Methods

Policy-based RL: REINFORCE, Actor-Critic, TRPO, and PPO.

Day 3 · 14 July

Continuous Control

RL for continuous action spaces and the reward design problem.

Day 4 · 15 July

Advanced RL Topics

Model-based, offline, inverse, multi-agent, and real-world reinforcement learning.
